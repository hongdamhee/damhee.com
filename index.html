About

I am a doctoral candidate in education, with professional experience in student services and higher education administration. My work examines how institutional systems and everyday campus experiences affect student belonging and care.

My dissertation focuses on how Korean American undergraduates interpret dining and food access in campus and local environments, and how cultural familiarity and meaning-making connect to belonging.
