Professional Experience

Overview

My professional experiences span domestic and international contexts across K–12 schools, community colleges, and public and private universities. Although my roles have varied in function and setting, the unifying theme across my career is a commitment to supporting students from all backgrounds and creating structures that bring their brilliance, voices, and strengths into focus. I intentionally position myself in spaces where I can help expand access, equity, and belonging while strengthening institutional practices that shape student success.
