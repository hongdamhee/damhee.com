Overview

My professional experience spans domestic and international contexts and includes work across K–12, community colleges, and private and public universities. Across roles in student services, international education, conduct and community standards, program operations, and student support, I have worked to help students navigate complex systems and access resources and opportunities. The unifying theme in my career is a commitment to positioning myself in roles where I can support and bring to light the brilliance of students from all backgrounds.
