Research

Overview

My research examines how culturally relevant food experiences relate to Asian American students’ sense of belonging in higher education. Through qualitative inquiry, I explore how memory, emotion, identity, and access shape students’ interpretations of campus environments. My work is grounded in culturally engaging campus environments (CECE) and Suda frameworks and centers Korean American undergraduate students at a minority-serving public university.
